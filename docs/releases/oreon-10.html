What's New in Oreon 10

Enhanced Desktop Experience

  • GNOME 47: Latest desktop environment with improved performance and new features

Known Issues & Workarounds

Graphics Driver Compatibility

Some older NVIDIA graphics cards may require manual driver installation. See our troubleshooting guide for assistance.

Wayland Display Server

Oreon 10 uses Wayland by default. If you experience issues with certain applications, you can switch to X11 from the login screen.

Upgrade Information
