Our Team

Meet the people who make Oreon Project possible

A passionate community of developers, designers, and enthusiasts working together to bring Enterprise Linux to the desktop.

Board of Directors

The visionaries behind Oreon Project

Brandon Lester

Project Lead

Brandon launched the Oreon Project in January 2024 with a vision to make Enterprise Linux more accessible for desktop users.

Ali Moumen

Community Lead

Ali manages both technical and non technical teams, most commonly community matters in order to ensure full operation of Oreon on all aspects. He initially joined Oreon Project in June 2024.

Our Teams

Specialized groups working together

Development Team

The development team is responsible for the development of Oreon, focusing on system integration, performance optimization, and feature implementation.

Community Teams

Oreon includes many other teams, such as the design team, the documentation team, and the social media team, all working to make Oreon better.
