Oreon Project Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Saturday the 19th April 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Oreon Project ("Oreon", "we", "us", "our"). The Oreon Project is part of BoostyConnect Technologies. BoostyConnect Technologies fully adheres to this Privacy Policy concerning all data related to the Oreon Project, which is managed by the same dedicated team. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in relation to the Oreon Operating System ("Oreon OS") and your use of the Oreon Project website (the "Website"), including downloading Oreon OS.

Your privacy is critically important to us.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect (Oreon OS)

The Oreon Operating System (Oreon OS) is designed with user privacy as a fundamental principle. Oreon OS itself does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data or personally identifiable information (PII) from your computer during its operation.

We do not monitor your usage, track your activities, or gather any data about the files, applications, or configurations on your system through the operating system itself.

3. Information We Collect (Website & Downloads)

When you visit our Website or download Oreon OS software, our web servers automatically collect limited, standard technical information necessary for the operation, security, and analysis of the Website. This information includes:

  • IP Address: The Internet Protocol address used to access our Website.
  • User Agent Information: Information provided by your web browser, such as browser type and version, and operating system type.
  • Download Details: The specific version of Oreon OS requested for download and the date/time of the request.
  • Referrer Information: The URL of the web page that linked you to our site, if any.

This information is collected via standard server logs.

External Services (Google Fonts)

Our Website uses Google Fonts to display text. When you access our Website, your web browser may download fonts directly from Google's servers. This process requires your browser to connect to Google, which may involve sending your IP address and browser information to Google.

Google's handling of this data is governed by its own privacy policy. We do not control Google's data collection or use. You can find more information on Google's policies on their website.

4. How We Use Collected Information

The limited information collected through our Website logs is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Website Operation and Security: To maintain the functionality, security, and integrity of our Website and download infrastructure, including diagnosing technical problems and preventing abuse (e.g., denial-of-service attacks).
  • Analytics: To understand general user trends, such as which versions of Oreon OS are being downloaded, approximate geographic distribution of downloads (based on anonymized IP data), and overall website traffic patterns. This helps us improve our services and prioritize development. Download counts are aggregated and do not identify individual users.
  • Bandwidth Management: To monitor and manage the bandwidth usage of our download servers.

5. Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, lease, trade, or otherwise share your personal information (including IP addresses or download logs) with any third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

We will only disclose log data if required to do so by law, court order, or other binding government request, and only to the extent necessary to comply with such legal obligation.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative security measures to protect the server log information we collect against loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, please be aware that no security measures are perfect or impenetrable.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Website and Oreon OS are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or a higher age threshold as required by applicable law, such as 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a minor without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. If you believe we might have any information from or about a minor, please contact us at the email address below.

8. International Users & Your Rights

The Oreon Project operates globally. The limited information we collect (server logs) may be stored and processed on servers located outside of your home country. We take steps to ensure that data is handled securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., EEA/UK, California), you may have certain rights regarding the limited personal information we hold about you (primarily your IP address in server logs). These rights may include the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us. We will respond to requests in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy on this page and revise the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our data practices.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: legal@oreonproject.org

Disclaimer: This Privacy Policy is provided for informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice. Please consult with a qualified legal professional for advice specific to your situation and jurisdiction.
